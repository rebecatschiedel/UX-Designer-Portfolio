Once upen a time...

...there was a coffee lover that decides to explore the world. She flavored food with feelings, colored paper with the light reflected in her lenses. She shared her experiences with others, but her curiosity was not satisfied.

She wanted to know how other people would experience the same situation. She wanted to feel through other’s feelings, see through other’s eyes. And then she begun a new adventure, full of challenges and rewards.

To me, UX design is to tell a story in a way that everyone will be able to understand.
