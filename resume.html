SEP 2020 - PRESENT, Volunteer
JavaScript, NextJS, SASS, Git/Github, Figma

The Web Dev Path is a team of professional developers project that aims to provide a comprehensive path for those who seek to begin their web development journey.

To do so, we're building a Next.js platform that will be a place to share ideas about web development and where Non-Profit Organizations can get help for their web products by sharing their project and needs and getting connected to volunteer junior web developers.

I've been working as a volunteer Front-End developer and UI/UX Designer. By collaborating on this initiative, I've been able to understand the practical approach of handling a codebase in a team environment.
